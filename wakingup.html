After Waking Up

After you wakeup, Drink 2 glasses of warm water with lime juice(1 lime) and 1 tablespoon apple cider vinegar and 1 tablespoon fresh aloe-vera and 1 tablespoon honey... Drink it slowly.



Add half teaspoon cinnamon powder and one-fourth teaspoon stevia for diabetics and skip the honey.
